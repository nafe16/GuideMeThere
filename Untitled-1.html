Explore Cox's Bazar

Discover the beauty, culture, and cuisine of this coastal gem.

Places to Visit

  • Cox's Bazar Beach: The world's longest natural sea beach, perfect for sunbathing and swimming.
  • Himchari National Park: A lush park with rainforests and waterfalls, ideal for nature lovers.
  • Aggmeda Khyang Monastery: A significant Buddhist monastery with ancient manuscripts.
  • Ramu Village: Known for its temples and traditional handicrafts.
  • Radiant Fish World: An aquarium showcasing diverse marine life.

Cultural Traditions

Cox's Bazar is a melting pot of cultures, with traditional music, dance, and festivals reflecting its rich heritage.

Local Cuisine

  • Fried Rupchanda: Deep-fried fish marinated with herbs and spices.
  • Loitta Fry: Fried loitta fish served with rice or salad.
  • Spicy Crab: Saltwater crabs cooked with a blend of spices.
  • Ilish Paturi: Hilsa fish wrapped in banana leaves and steamed.
  • Assorted Bhortas: Mashed seafood preparations seasoned with spices.

Accommodation

  • Sayeman Beach Resort: Luxury resort with stunning sea views.
  • Army Welfare Trust Rest House Joltarongo: Managed by the Bangladesh Army, offering high security.
  • Sea Pearl Beach Resort & Spa: Luxurious rooms and a water park.
  • Seagull Hotels Ltd.: Five-star hotel near the beach.
  • Mermaid Beach Resort: Serene resort with private beach access.

Whether you're seeking relaxation, cultural exploration, or local flavors, Cox's Bazar has something for everyone.
